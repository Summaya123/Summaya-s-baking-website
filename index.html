About the Founder

SUMMAYA ASIF

Summaya Asif is a baker,chef, and cookbook author. Since 2011, she has been sharing meticulously tested recipes and step-by-step tutorials, helping home bakers gain confidence in the kitchen. Over the years, her dedication to approachable baking has built a loyal community of millions. Her work has been featured on Good Morning Pakistan , in People Magazine, and on popular sites like BuzzFeed, HuffPost, The Kitchn, and Country Living.
